Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important. Boztik Creative Toolkit has been designed to respect your privacy and collect as little information as possible.

Information We Collect

Boztik Creative Toolkit does not collect, store or sell your personal information.

The extension does not require account creation, does not display advertising and does not use tracking technologies.

Permissions Explained

💾

Storage

Used only to save your extension preferences, statistics and achievements locally on your own device.

🖼️

Downloads

Used only when you choose to download an image using the Download Original feature.

🌍

Host Permissions

Required so the extension can analyze images available on webpages that you choose to interact with.

Your Privacy Comes First

Boztik Creative Toolkit has one goal: to help creators work faster while respecting user privacy. No tracking. No hidden analytics. No unnecessary data collection.
